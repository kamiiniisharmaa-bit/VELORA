We believe in objects that carry the energy of the cosmos.

Velora was born from a simple idea: that everyday objects can be both functional and soulful. A ceramic mug should feel like holding a piece of the moon. A journal should spark wonder. We exist to bridge minimalist design with celestial storytelling — creating pieces that resonate with dreamers, minimalists, and seekers of the extraordinary.

Every Velora piece is thoughtfully crafted using sustainable materials, guided by curiosity, and delivered with intention. We are a community of artists, designers, and stargazers who believe that small rituals bring magic back to modern life.

— Luna Chen, Founder & Creative Director

Our mission: Curiosity over clutter

In a world of fast production, Velora champions slow, intentional design. We work with ethical artisans, low‑impact materials, and timeless forms. Our goal is to inspire a deeper connection to the objects we interact with daily — turning the mundane into a ritual.

From a sketchbook to a constellation ✨

Velora launched in 2021 as a tiny online studio run from a coastal apartment. What started as hand‑thrown ceramics and vintage celestial prints has grown into a curated destination for mindful living. Yet our heart remains the same: each product is designed to spark a moment of awe — whether it's the way light falls on a vase or the texture of a linen throw.

Today, Velora collaborates with independent makers across the globe, from Kyoto ceramicists to Oaxacan textile weavers. We are not a mass brand; we are a collective of dreamers who believe in quality, meaning, and the beauty of the unknown.

Flagship studio: San Francisco · Kyoto · Melbourne (digital-first)

"Velora is not about owning more — it's about feeling more. Every object we release is a little nudge to look up at the stars and remember that we are part of something vast. That's why we design with the moon in mind and the earth at heart."

— Jamie Rivera, Co-founder & Head of Design
